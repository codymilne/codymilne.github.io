Cody's Fresh PC

Now, this is a story all about how,

My laptop got flipped-turned upside down.

And, I'd like to take a minute and jot this down here,

and tell all about how I built my new PC with care.

In Frys electronics born and raised,

Watching Linus Tech Tips was where I spent most of my days.

Liquid cooling, overclocking, thermal paste, all cool,

And all to get better FPS when I got home from school.

When a couple of games who were memory hoards,

Started making trouble on my motherboard.

I got one little bluscreen and but it had me scared,

I said, its time to upgrade all components, Devil may care.

I Saved and penny pinched day after day,

But GPU's could not be bought anyway.

I tried the Newegg lottery daily for a winning ticket,

and won a spot for RTX 3080 and said, I will get it.

Then on to ram looking for something fast,

5200 hertz, DDR5 is part of the master class.

But wait, now my PSU cant power all that,

Quick Amazon, I will need 1200 watts, Stat.

All my parts arrived quick on Next day air,

Hope my old monitor can handle this PC built with care.
